Privacy Policy

Your Data Safety

At SheGO, your safety and privacy are our top priorities. We only collect data that helps us provide a secure ride-hailing experience for women.

Location Tracking

We track your location during trips to ensure your ride is monitored by our safety team and shared with your emergency contacts if needed.

Location data is only shared with PDRM in case of an SOS emergency.

Identity Protection

Your phone number is masked when communicating with drivers. Drivers will only see your first name and pickup/drop-off points.

Data Usage

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. Data is used solely for improving SheGO services and maintaining high safety standards.
